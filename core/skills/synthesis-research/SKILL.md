# Synthesis Research

Synthesis Research handles cross-source reasoning. It is deliberately gated:
source impacts become candidates or review items before they update multiple
formal pages.

## Modes

- `fanout-review`: stage source impact in `maintenance/fanout_candidates.md` and
  optionally promote a reviewable proposal to `maintenance/review_queue.md`.
- `apply-approved-fanout`: apply one explicitly approved review item to formal
  wiki pages while preserving evidence tier and confidence.
- `thesis-review`: create a thesis run under `maintenance/thesis_runs/` with
  supporting, opposing, mechanistic, meta-review, adjacent evidence, evidence
  table, and verdict proposal.
- `synthesis-page-start`: create a draft synthesis or project-synthesis page and
  a Codex discussion prompt.
- `external-sandbox-sync`: prepare a same-computer prompt for another sandbox to
  update this exact database path with clear evidence boundaries.

## Rules

- Do not let one paper silently rewrite concepts, synthesis, overview, or hot
  pages without fan-out review or explicit Save.
- Preserve counter-evidence needs and supersession relationships.
- Thesis verdicts are proposals until saved through Knowledge Workbench or
  approved fan-out.
- Project history and meeting decisions belong in project synthesis/meetings,
  not ordinary literature synthesis.
