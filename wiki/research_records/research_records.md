## Rule

Every future search/ingest session should create at least two records when applicable:

- `search_ingest_<topic>_<YYYY-MM-DD>.md`

If a request contains multiple research themes, split the question records and search ingest records by theme. Use a bridge synthesis page only for cross-theme coupling.
