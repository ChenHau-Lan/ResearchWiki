## Search Notes

  PDF QC.
- RKF `rkf-knowledge-synthesis` is used only after PDF QC to create paper pages
  or project overview pages.
- Candidate status does not authorize a wiki paper page. Only QCed PDFs or
  legal publisher artifacts can be promoted into durable wiki pages.
