## Summary

TAHOPE is the Taiwan-Area Heavy rain Observation and Prediction Experiment,
planned as a Taiwan-led intensive observation and prediction experiment for
Mei-yu fronts, mesoscale convective systems, and landfalling typhoons near
Taiwan. Its design connects Taiwan facilities with U.S. and Japanese
collaborations, including PRECIP and T-PARCII.

## Observation Design Signals

- Combine operational networks with research instruments.
- Use radar, radiosonde, dropsonde, aircraft, wind profiler, and satellite data.
- Treat real-time or near-real-time data assimilation as part of the experiment,
  not as an afterthought.
- Link field science to operational QPE/QPF and disaster decision support.
