# Research Wiki Purpose

This wiki exists to compile research evidence into durable, reviewable
knowledge. It is not a PDF folder, a chat transcript archive, or a raw RAG
index. It is a source-first knowledge runtime where `raw/` preserves evidence,
`wiki/` preserves curated understanding, and `maintenance/` preserves review
state.

## What It Optimizes For

- Preserve source provenance before automation convenience.
- Make claims easy to inspect, challenge, and supersede.
- Keep Query read-only and Save explicit.
- Route uncertain or multi-page impact through review before it becomes stable
  wiki knowledge.

## Not For

- Unauthorized source acquisition.
- Publishing private raw PDFs or full text.
- Turning one source into broad synthesis without review.
- Treating an LLM answer as evidence without source links.
