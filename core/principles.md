# RKF Principles

1. Evidence outlives conversation.
2. Candidates are not evidence.
3. ARS output is not evidence.
4. Private PDFs stay outside public Git.
5. Paper wiki pages come from QCed PDF evidence.
6. Durable article text is not a data layer.
7. Topics prevent discovery drift.
8. Graph links make memory inspectable.
9. Lint reports and plans; it does not silently rewrite.
