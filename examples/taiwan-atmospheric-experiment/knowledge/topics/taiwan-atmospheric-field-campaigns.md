# Taiwan Atmospheric Field Campaigns

## Scope

Taiwan atmospheric observation campaigns and field studies involving terrain
rainfall, monsoon flow, aerosol, cloud microphysics, CCN, radar, sounding,
lidar, or in-situ measurements.

## Include

- TAMEX and terrain/Mei-yu precipitation observations.
- SoWMEX/TiMREX rainfall and microphysics observations.
- TAHOPE/PRECIP severe rainfall, radar, aircraft, and data-assimilation observations.
- Cape Fuguei aerosol and CCN field observations.
- Lulin aerosol-cloud microphysics observations.
- Taiwan complex-terrain precipitation and local circulation studies.

## Exclude

- Pure global modeling with no Taiwan observation component.
- Non-atmospheric environmental monitoring.
- News-only claims without paper, official data, or project documentation.

- Taiwan atmospheric field experiment TAMEX DOI

- TAHOPE PRECIP Taiwan heavy rain experiment DOI
